How to Play
A guide to the ancient streets
⚔ Basic Moves

· Move with ←/→ or A/D. Hold SHIFT to sprint.

· Jump with SPACE (or W/↑). Hold for a higher jump. Tap again in the air for a double jump.

· Duck with ↓ to slip under arrows or enter green pipes.

· Slide along walls to perform a wall-jump.

🔥 Power-ups

· Fireball — press F to launch a fireball.

· Speed · Super Jump · Magnet · Shield · Star — all timed buffs.

🪙 Tips

· 50 coins or 10 gems give an extra life.

· Stomp enemies back-to-back for combo points.

· Pipes warp to bonus rooms. Switches and hidden blocks open shortcuts.

· Press ESC any time to pause.
